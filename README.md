
# Car Racing Game

This is a thrilling racing game built in Unity, featuring multiple race tracks, enemy AI cars, a car selection screen, and a polished UI for a smooth player experience. Players can select their car, compete against AI-driven opponents, and aim for the top spot on the leaderboard.

Features:  
**Player Car Controller:** A custom car controller script for realistic driving dynamics, enhanced with physics-based movement.

**Enemy AI Controllers:** Adaptive AI controllers for opponent cars. I originally learned to build a car controller from YouTube tutorials, then modified it to serve as an AI controller for enemy cars, giving them pathfinding and competitive behaviors.

**Car Selection:** Players can choose their preferred car model before the race, adding personalization to the gameplay experience.

**Dynamic UI:** A clean and interactive UI system, including a leaderboard, lap counter, and timer, designed to make the game easy to navigate and engaging for players.
**Race Tracks:** Multiple race tracks to keep the gameplay fresh and challenging.

**Objective:** Choose your car, race against AI-controlled opponents, and aim to finish first. Each race is timed, and your best scores are saved on the leaderboard.
Controls: Use standard racing controls (e.g., WASD/Arrow keys for movement, spacebar for brakes).

**Difficulty:** The AI difficulty is balanced to provide a challenging yet fair competition.  

Learning Journey
I developed the car controller mechanics by following a YouTube tutorial and then significantly enhanced it to work with enemy AI, adapting the logic for pathfinding and autonomous movement. This experience allowed me to create challenging AI opponents that add excitement and competition to the races.
